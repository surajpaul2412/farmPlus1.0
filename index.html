Our Journey: Cultivating Growth as We Grow

Brought into business in 2011, Farm Plus is the Output domain of DeHaat that provides superior quality Dry and Fresh products, picked up right from the fields and delivered at the doorsteps of clients across the country.

With 4,00,000 MT of fresh and dry produce processed yearly and almost 8000 agri orders per day, Farm Plus has become one of India’s largest distributors and importers of these commodities.

By utilizing the best of technology, strong sourcing methods and highly trained professionals in the system Farm Plus is on its way to a rapid expansion with a consistent aim of providing faster, better and higher quality fresh and dry produce.

Our Values: Ensuring Optimum Services

At Farm Plus, we focus on simplifying and improving the supply chain of agri-products to enable better, faster and more efficient fulfilment of all the demands from our customers. We work on 6 core values that allow us to deliver the best of services.
Efficient Supply Chain
Streamlining business operations to achieve optimum results at minimized costs and time.
Superior Technology Solutions
Increasing productivity across channels to enable faster, better and higher profitability.
Premium Quality
Offering yields of the highest standard to bring an increase in client business and customer satisfaction.
Ample Commodity Choices
Providing a huge variety of products to fulfil demands throughout the year and across the nation.
Empowered Farmers
Enabling transparent methods of payment and procurement to strengthen the livelihoods and financial status of farmers.
Consistent Business Growth
Ensuring year-round availability of products and services to provide steady and upward expansion of businesses.
