Pomegranate

Pomegranate

Pomegranate is one of the major commercially grown fruit crops in India.Cultivated largely in Maharashtra with small scale plantations spread across Gujarat, Rajasthan, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu , Andhra Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Punjab and Haryana, the fruit ensures higer returns and profitibility.
Pomegranates are available all year in India, but are exported between August to October and then again between January to March.This fruit is further in demand in the juice, jam, squash and syrup industry.
